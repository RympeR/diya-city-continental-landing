About iC

itContinental is a reliable partner in the field of innovative technologies. The company's product portfolio
includes the latest solutions and software from global manufacturers, the vast majority of which come from
the countries of the European Union and the United States of America, as well as own developments.

The main areas of activity are the implementation of software for IT services, technical support of critical
business systems and infrastructure, and other specialized services. Clients are enterprises of key sectors of
the economy, such as the financial sector, energy, metallurgy, retail and others.

The company, together with partners, provides organizations with access to the latest global developments
and technologies, accelerates the digital transformation of enterprises.
